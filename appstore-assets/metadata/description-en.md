### Full Description

**The Smart Vocabulary App for IELTS Preparation**

EarWords is a professional IELTS vocabulary learning tool based on the authoritative "IELTS Vocabulary Bible" by Liu Hongbo. With 3,674 high-frequency IELTS words and the proven SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm, EarWords helps you memorize efficiently and ace your IELTS exam.

**【Scientific Memorization】**
- Powered by the SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm
- 5-level rating system based on your recall accuracy
- Personalized review schedule tailored to your memory
- Customizable daily new words and review quotas

**【Audio Learning - "Ear Training"】**
- Convert words + example sentences into audio
- Three playback modes: Sequential, Random, Spaced Repeat
- Adjustable speech rate for different proficiency levels
- Background playback support - learn while commuting or exercising

**【Comprehensive Word Bank】**
- 22 chapters covering all IELTS topics
- Natural Geography, Education, Space Exploration, Animal Conservation...
- Practical example sentences for contextual learning
- Complete phonetic symbols, definitions, and usage

**【Progress Tracking & Motivation】**
- Real-time learning statistics and progress tracking
- Streak counter to build consistent study habits
- Visual learning trend charts
- Chapter progress tracking for systematic preparation

**【iCloud Sync】**
- Automatic sync across all your devices
- Seamless learning experience on iPhone and iPad

**Perfect for:**
- IELTS test takers
- English vocabulary learners
- Busy professionals learning on the go

Start your IELTS vocabulary breakthrough today. Make vocabulary learning enjoyable!
